About Our App

We are Second Year (BE) students & have made this Image Classification App using TFLite.
Capture & classify flowers around your Surroundings.

How The App Works?

Scan

After the installation, ALLOW the camera access in your device. Then, place the camera in front of the flower or plant you want to classify.

Capture

Once you hold the camera in front of it, capture it.

That's It

The resultant category / flower name along with it's basic information will appear.
Enjoy.
